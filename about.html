About Ram Gill

Ram is a Vancouver native and specialises in helping business owners create a work environment and culture where their team feels loved, cared for, and supported so they can deliver business results.

Ram brings 25 years of senior HR experience working for great companies, 1000s of leaders, 100s Teams. Ram holds a Chartered Professional Human Resources (CPHR) Designation and in 2018 he was the CPHR (BC/Yukon) HR Professional of the Year Finalist. Youtube Video Link.

Ram’s clients describe him as a reliable helper who is caring and fun. He is non-judgemental and treats you and your business like you matter. He makes you feel safe, supported, and more confident.

Ram is someone who:

✔ genuinely cares about people
✔ wants to help create great workplaces
✔ wants to see people happy in their work and personal life
✔ wants people winning

Ram’s leadership philosophy is very simple. If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go as a team. This only happens by building strong relationships based on trust, effective communication, being involved, mutual respect, positive intent, accountability, and continual focus on a winning culture.
